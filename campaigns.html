Why we walk

900 miles. One building. One neighborhood.

Walking is a deliberate choice. It's slow. It's visible. It forces the conversation — in every town between Chicago and New York — about what's happening on the South Side and what's possible when a community refuses to be overlooked.

Every dollar raised by Walk Across America goes directly to the LEO Center capital campaign. No overhead deduction. Audited annually.
