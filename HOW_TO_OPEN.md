# Project H.O.O.D. — clickable prototype

This folder is a **static preview** of the redesigned projecthood.org. It is **not** the live site and nothing here is connected to donations, forms, email, or a CMS yet. The goal is for the team to click through every page, flag what's wrong or missing, and send a consolidated round of edits back.

## How to open it

1. Open this folder on your computer.
2. Double-click `index.html`.
3. It will open in your default browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, etc.).
4. Use the top nav and in-page links to move around. Everything should open locally without internet — except webfonts (Google Fonts) and any external link that points out (Tiltify, NetworkForGood, Google Forms).

## What's in here

- 15 pages: Home, About, Programs hub, Program detail (Violence Prevention used as the template), Impact, LEO Center, Campaigns (WAA), Get Involved, Donate, Volunteer, Events, Partner, News, Contact, 404
- One stylesheet (`css/styles.css`) — all brand colors, fonts, buttons, components live here
- One JS file (`js/main.js`) — mobile nav toggle + active-nav highlight
- Brand assets in `img/` (logo + acronym variants)

## What is a placeholder vs. real

**Live now (in prototype):**
- All page layouts, nav, footer, brand colors, typography, content structure
- Page-to-page links
- External links out to Tiltify, NetworkForGood, Apricot (these will really open the destination)

**Placeholders (intentionally stubbed):**
- Photos — dashed stripe boxes labeled "image placeholder"
- Donate form — shows a styled block that says "NetworkForGood embed goes here"
- Volunteer / Contact / Partner forms — same, styled block that says "Google Form embed goes here"
- Events list — shows a static sample; the real site will pull from a Google Calendar
- News posts — 6 sample cards with lorem-style titles
- Newsletter signup — styled input, not wired to NetworkForGood yet

## How to send feedback

Best way: click through every page and leave comments in a single Google Doc or email back to Brian. Helpful format:

- Page filename (e.g., `about.html`)
- Section name (e.g., "Our story timeline")
- What's wrong or missing

Flag anything: wrong numbers, wrong names, wrong photos (or where photos should go), wording, button labels, missing sections, anything that belongs on the old site that isn't here.

## What happens next

After this round of edits, we move from prototype → real GitHub Pages site with Decap CMS (for staff updates) and Google Calendar (for events). The prototype becomes the visual reference; the content you correct here is the content that ships.
