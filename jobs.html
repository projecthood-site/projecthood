About working here

Why people join Project H.O.O.D.

Community-first

We hire people who know Woodlawn or want to become part of it. Proximity to the community isn't a perk — it's the job.

Real impact

No national overhead. Every dollar — and every hour — goes toward youth, families, and the block. You see your work matter.

Cross-program work

Our five programs are interlocking, not siloed. Staff collaborate across violence prevention, workforce, education, and more.

Don't see the right role?

We sometimes hire for positions we haven't posted yet. If you're rooted in Woodlawn and believe in this work, reach out.
