Recovery in the H.O.O.D.

Recovery is not a destination — it’s a journey best traveled together. Whatever you’re carrying, you don’t have to carry it alone. We walk with you, step by step.

What it is

A community that walks with you.

Recovery in the H.O.O.D. is a peer-led support community for neighbors working through addiction, trauma, and the hard road back to wholeness. It was built on a simple truth: healing happens faster, and lasts longer, when no one has to do it alone.

Here you’ll find people who understand — because many of them have walked the same road. There’s no judgment and no cost. Just honest support, real accountability, and a room full of people pulling for you.

We meet people wherever they are on the journey: someone taking a first tentative step, someone rebuilding after a setback, or someone years into recovery who wants to give back. Every story is welcome.

How we gather

Weekly meetings. Monthly Speakerthons.

🗓

Weekly meetings

Every week, our Recovery in the H.O.O.D. meetings bring neighbors together to support one another, share openly, and find strength in community — a safe space for healing, accountability, and hope. Come as you are.

🎤

Monthly Speakerthons

Once a month we host a Speakerthon — an open gathering where people in recovery share their testimonies out loud. These nights remind everyone in the room that change is possible and that recovery is worth fighting for.

Want the next meeting or Speakerthon date? Reach out or call 773-923-8270 and we’ll get you the details.

Getting to treatment

Referrals — and a hand to hold along the way.

When someone needs more than peer support, we connect them to trusted treatment facilities and care providers. But we don’t just hand you a phone number and wish you luck.

We walk with you step by step — helping you understand your options, get to intake, navigate the paperwork, and stay connected through the ups and downs. Our goal isn’t only to get you into treatment; it’s to make sure you’re still supported when you come home.

The support

What walking with you looks like.

  • Referrals to trusted treatment facilities and providers
  • Step-by-step help getting to intake and through the paperwork
  • Weekly peer support meetings & group circles
  • Monthly Speakerthons — testimony, encouragement, community
  • Ongoing connection and accountability after treatment

Ready to take the first step?

Reach out today. We’ll meet you where you are and walk with you from there.
